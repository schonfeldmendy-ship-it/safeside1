About Safeside

A family of protection, built one relationship at a time.

Our story

Founded in 2007 on a simple promise.

Safeside Insurance was built on the belief that protecting your family or business shouldn’t be slow, confusing, or impersonal. What began as one agent determined to answer the phone faster than anyone else has grown into a nationwide agency serving more than 10,000 clients.

As an independent agency, we aren’t tied to a single carrier. We shop the top-rated insurers on your behalf, match you with the right coverage at a competitive rate, and stay in your corner when it matters most — at claim time.
